Hours of Operations/Ordering

Milk’d Milkshakes is open on various days of the week due to owner schedules and we are open from 8pm till midnight. More consistent hours will be announced soon! Follow us on instagram at @milkdmilkshakes or click the instagram logo down below for announcements of our openings!

To order a milkshake all you have to do is this number during our hours of operation. 725-221-7656 Please be sure to include your name for the order, your order and your payment method (Venmo or Cash) on your text to complete your order. Once texted, your order should be ready in the next 5-15 minutes based on the amount of orders we have. Thank you for choosing Milk’d Milkshakes!
